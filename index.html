Log pods - Glamping at West Middlewick Farm

Glamping has come to West Middlewick! Situated in the camping field, we now have two cosy little log-pods available for hire, perfect for a weekend away in the countryside without the need for a tent.

Each log pod has a 4' 6" futon as well as a 11' long hammock - suitable for one or two children. The log pods both heated and well insulated provide a perfect little snug in the countryside. These are simply cosy shelters, so other than a bed to sleep on, which is waiting for you when you get here, you need to bring everything else you might need with you. So that means bring your duvet or sleeping bag, pillows and a sheet. All we offer is a warm roof over your head and a few moos while you sleep and pictures of sheep to help you nod off.

We now have a covered BBQ area and picnic area for those staying in the log pods.
